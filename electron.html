Electron

The fundamental subatomic particle responsible for chemical bonding and electricity.

Properties of Electrons

Electrons are negatively charged particles with a very small mass. They orbit the nucleus and are fundamental to the structure of atoms.

Electron Behavior

Electrons exhibit both wave and particle properties, allowing them to form orbitals and participate in quantum phenomena like tunneling.

Applications

Electrons are essential in electricity, semiconductors, and technologies like electron microscopes and particle accelerators.
