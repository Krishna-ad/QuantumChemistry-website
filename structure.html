Structure of an Atom

Unveiling the fundamental building blocks of matter: protons, neutrons, and electrons.

Nucleus

The dense center of the atom, containing protons and neutrons, where most of the atom's mass is concentrated.

Electrons

Negatively charged particles that orbit the nucleus, defining the atom's chemical properties.

Atomic Orbitals

Regions around the nucleus where electrons are likely to be found, each with a unique shape and energy level.
