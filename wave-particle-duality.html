Wave-Particle Duality

Understanding the dual nature of light and matter, acting as both particles and waves.

What is Wave-Particle Duality?

Wave-particle duality is the concept that every particle or quantum entity can exhibit both wave-like and particle-like behavior. This phenomenon is central to quantum mechanics.

Double-Slit Experiment

The double-slit experiment demonstrated wave-particle duality by showing that light and electrons can create interference patterns, a behavior typical of waves, while also acting as particles.

Applications

Wave-particle duality plays a vital role in quantum technologies such as electron microscopes, quantum computing, and even the study of photons in optical devices.
