Quantum Particles

The building blocks of matter and the cornerstone of quantum mechanics.

What Are Quantum Particles?

Quantum particles include subatomic particles like electrons, protons, and neutrons, as well as fundamental particles such as quarks and leptons, governed by the principles of quantum mechanics.

Behavior of Quantum Particles

Quantum particles exhibit wave-particle duality, entanglement, and superposition, defying classical intuitions about the nature of matter.

Applications

The study of quantum particles underpins fields like quantum computing, particle physics, and advanced material science.
