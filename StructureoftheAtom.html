Structure of the Atom

Introduction

An atom is the basic unit of matter that defines the chemical elements. Atoms are composed of three fundamental subatomic particles: protons, neutrons, and electrons.

Subatomic Particles

  • Protons: Positively charged particles located in the nucleus of the atom.
  • Neutrons: Neutral particles also found in the nucleus that contribute to the atom's mass.
  • Electrons: Negatively charged particles that orbit the nucleus in different energy levels or shells.
