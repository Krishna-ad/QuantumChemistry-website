Wave Properties

Exploring the fundamental properties that describe the behavior of all waves, from sound to light.

Types of Waves

Waves can be classified as mechanical (like sound) or electromagnetic (like light). Mechanical waves require a medium, while electromagnetic waves can travel through a vacuum.

Characteristics of Waves

Key properties of waves include wavelength, frequency, amplitude, and wave speed. These properties help us understand the energy and behavior of waves.

Wave Interference

When two or more waves meet, they can interfere with each other, either amplifying or canceling out. This phenomenon is essential in understanding wave behavior in various contexts.
