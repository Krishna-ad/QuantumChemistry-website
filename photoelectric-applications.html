Applications of the Photoelectric Effect

Exploring the practical uses of the photoelectric effect in modern technology and everyday life.

Solar Panels

Solar panels use the photoelectric effect to convert light into electricity. Photons from sunlight knock electrons loose in semiconductor materials, generating electric current.

Photoelectric Sensors

Photoelectric sensors are used in a wide range of applications, from automatic doors to industrial equipment, where they detect the presence of objects by measuring light levels.

Light Meters

Light meters in photography and cinematography use the photoelectric effect to measure light intensity, helping photographers and videographers adjust their equipment for optimal exposure.
